TV Tracker

This is a Ruby on Rails app designed to allow users to keep track of their favorite television shows. It stores series, season, and episode information and allows users to add television shows to their tracking list. If the series the user wants to follow is not currently in the database, they can add the series by entering the title, at which point the app uses Nokogiri to scrape series, season, and episode information from Imdb, and adds it to the database.
